**Google:**
```bash
GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID="xxx.apps.googleusercontent.com"
GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET="GOCSPX-xxxx"
GOOGLE_REDIRECT_URI="https://yourdomain.com/api/auth/google/callback"
```
