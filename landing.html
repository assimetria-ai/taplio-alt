FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you start growing.

Will the AI posts sound like me?
Yes. Taplio's AI learns from your existing LinkedIn posts to match your tone, vocabulary, and style. The more you use Taplio, the better it gets at sounding exactly like you — so readers won't know the difference.
Does Taplio post automatically to LinkedIn?
Yes. Once you approve and schedule a post, Taplio publishes it automatically at your chosen time — or at the AI-recommended peak time for your audience. You stay in full control of what goes live.
Is this against LinkedIn's terms of service?
Taplio uses LinkedIn's official API for all scheduling and publishing. Your account is safe. Hundreds of thousands of creators use LinkedIn scheduling tools — it's a well-established and accepted practice.
Can I manage multiple LinkedIn profiles?
Yes, with the Agency plan you can manage up to 5 LinkedIn profiles from one dashboard, with separate content calendars, analytics, and team access for each account.
What happens if I cancel?
You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that, you drop to the Free plan — your posts, analytics history, and settings are preserved. You never lose your data.
Can I try Pro before paying?
Yes. Start on the Free plan with no credit card. When you're ready to upgrade, Pro includes a 14-day free trial so you can experience the full feature set before you're charged.
